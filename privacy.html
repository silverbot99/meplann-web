Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

MePlann respects your privacy. We prioritize local data storage and transparency regarding AI interactions. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Personal Information: MePlann stores your profile, tasks, and journal entries locally on your device using Apple's Core Data and UserDefaults. We do not maintain a central server database of your personal thoughts.
  • AI Interactions: Strictly necessary text inputs are sent to OpenAI (our API provider) to generate responses. We do not use your data to train public AI models.
  • Health Data: If enabled, we may write "Mindful Minutes" to Apple HealthKit. This data stays within your device ecosystem.

3. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI for intelligence features and Apple StoreKit for subscription payments. We do not have access to your credit card details.

4. Your Rights

You can delete all local data by deleting the app or using the reset options within the app settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at support@meplann.com.
