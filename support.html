Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the MePlann app > Profile Tab > Scroll down to "Subscription" and tap "Restore Purchase".

Are my journals private?

Absolutely. MePlann is a "Local First" app. Your text journals are stored on your device and in your personal iCloud. We do not have access to them.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete your account and all local data by going to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This action is irreversible.
